We're Hiring!

Join our team and be part of something special. We're looking for passionate individuals to help us create the perfect escape for our customers.

Barista

Salary: $28,000 - $35,000/year

We're looking for experienced baristas who are passionate about coffee and customer service. You'll be the face of Café Oasis, crafting perfect drinks and creating memorable experiences.

  • 2+ years barista experience
  • Excellent customer service skills
  • Knowledge of espresso-based drinks
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment

Shift Manager

Salary: $35,000 - $45,000/year

Lead our team during your shifts and ensure every customer leaves happy. You'll manage staff, handle inventory, and maintain our high standards of quality and service.

  • 3+ years café or restaurant experience
  • Proven leadership skills
  • Strong organizational abilities
  • Experience with POS systems

Pastry Chef

Salary: $32,000 - $42,000/year

Create delicious pastries and desserts that complement our coffee offerings. You'll work in our kitchen to produce fresh baked goods daily.

  • Culinary degree or equivalent experience
  • Knowledge of pastry techniques
  • Attention to detail
  • Food safety certification

Customer Service Associate

Salary: $26,000 - $32,000/year

Provide exceptional customer service and support our team. Handle customer inquiries, process transactions, and maintain a welcoming atmosphere.

  • Excellent communication skills
  • Friendly and outgoing personality
  • Ability to multitask
  • Previous customer service experience preferred

Marketing Specialist

Salary: $38,000 - $48,000/year

Help us grow our brand and reach new customers. You'll manage our social media, create marketing campaigns, and develop strategies to increase brand awareness.

  • 2+ years marketing experience
  • Social media expertise
  • Creative thinking
  • Analytics and data interpretation skills

Interested in joining our team?

Send your resume and a brief cover letter to: careers@cafeoasis.com

We look forward to hearing from you!
